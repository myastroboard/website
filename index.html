Privacy & Ownership

Your observatory. Your infrastructure. Your data.

myAstroBoard runs on your own server, NAS, mini-PC, or home lab. No account required, no forced cloud, no telemetry by default, no data selling, and no vendor lock-in. You decide what is exposed and what stays private.

  • Data stays local by default
  • No user tracking cookies on this website
  • No third-party analytics scripts
  • No subscription gatekeeping core features
